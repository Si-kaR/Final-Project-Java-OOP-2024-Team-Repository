           Climate Change in Ghana
•Climate change in Ghana is causing significant impacts on the country's natural and economic environments.
•Rising temperatures and unpredictable rainfall patterns affect farming and fishing, crucial sectors for the nation's food security and economy.
•Sea-level rise threatens coastal areas, leading to increased salinity and potential displacement of communities. 
•Hydropower, which provides over half of Ghana's electricity, is at risk due to changing water levels, affecting the power supply.
•Additionally, health issues such as malaria and cholera are expected to rise due to shifting water conditions.
